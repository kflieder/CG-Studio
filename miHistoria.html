At the age of 16, I stumbled upon the world of hairstyling, and it's been an incredible journey since.

My passion for this art led me to join a Loreal academy at the age of 18, where I gained invaluable experience.

Years later, I became a certified colorist with Redken, and opened my own salon in Monterrey.

In 2019, I decided to move to Mérida, where I rented a small space in a beautiful house, which has since grown into our current location.

We're proud to be an Avyna product retailer, and this partnership has allowed us to expand our offerings and services. Our success led us to move into a new plaza and revamp our space, and we're excited to continue growing and evolving.
